# PRODUCT.md - WebTinTuc

## Brand & Purpose
WebTinTuc is a premium, high-performance Vietnamese news aggregator specializing in game reviews, anime news, technology trends, movies, and general education.

## Target Audience
- Vietnamese youth, gamers, tech enthusiasts, and general readers who demand instant navigation, clean visuals, and an ad-supported but non-intrusive content layout.

## Register
- product

## Core Design Guidelines
- Dominant colors: Brand Red (`#df3232`), clean white backgrounds, and dark gray text.
- Layout: Balanced 970px width centered layout. Responsive column layouts (650px left column for articles, 300px right column for sidebars on desktop).
- Spacing: Consistently aligned with the header and footer margins.
